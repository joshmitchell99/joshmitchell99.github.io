To building a world where mental health is a priority

Welcome to Snapdragon! We're so happy you're interested in joining us. Here at Snapdragon, mental health professionals have the opportunity to put their unique skillset to use with communities that need their help.

<

How you can help:

Join us as a supervisor

If you are a licensed social worker or therapist, join our team providing supervisional hours to our associates. By donating supervisional hours you are helping underserved clients access the mental healthcare they deserve.

Join us as an associate

Get supervised clinical hours or community service hours (for free!) by providing counseling to our clients.

Join us as a partner

If you are in the mental health field but are looking for a different or less time consuming way to get engaged - join us as a partner and provide online webinars, one-time talks, group sessions, or other support for one of our many ongoing projects.

Please fill out this form if you are interested in working with us, and someone from our team will contact you promptly. We're so excited you're here!

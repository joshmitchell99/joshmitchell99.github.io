About

<

Snapdragon Health is a not-for-profit with a mission of making mental healthcare accessible to everyone.

<

Over 50% of Americans will be diagnosed with a mental health condition at some point during their lifetime, but under half will receive the help they deserve. While it’s common to struggle with mental health, resources are often difficult to find or incredibly expensive, with a typical session of therapy coming in at around two hundred dollars.

<

At Snapdragon, we appreciate the need for simple, effective, and affordable mental health resources, and we’re determined to bring them to everyone, regardless of their ability to pay. We do this by partnering with therapy and social worker associates, who are seeking supervised clinical hours in order to achieve their licensing. Thanks to the generosity of already licensed mental health professionals, we are able to pair associates with professionals willing to supervise. Then, using telemedicine, we connect these groups of counselors to people seeking affordable mental health care. We also provide more other mental health resources such as group therapy, art therapy, webinars, and group mindfulness sessions.

<

Often our clients are from low-income or disenfranchised populations. We are passionate about bringing mental healthcare to everyone - and fervently believe a healthy mind is a right - not a privilege. That’s why we operate entirely on a pay-what-you-can model, and all we ask is that our clients are ready to work towards a better, happier state of mind.

<

Aside: At Snapdragon we also partner with pre-existing organizations and other not-for-profits to provide therapy and counseling to an at-need clientele they already serve. If this sounds like you, please contact us by email about forming a partnership.
